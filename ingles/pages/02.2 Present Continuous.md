
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The Present Continuous is used for actions happening now, temporary situations, and planned future arrangements.
>
> **Español:** El Presente Continuo se usa para acciones que están ocurriendo ahora, situaciones temporales y planes futuros ya organizados.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I am studying English.
> - She is working right now.
> - They are playing soccer.
> - We are meeting our friends tomorrow.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + Verb To Be + Verb-ing + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am studying English. | Estoy estudiando inglés. |
| You are reading a book. | Estás leyendo un libro. |
| He is playing soccer. | Él está jugando fútbol. |
| She is cooking dinner. | Ella está cocinando la cena. |
| We are watching TV. | Estamos viendo televisión. |
| They are working. | Ellos están trabajando. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + Verb To Be + not + Verb-ing**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am not sleeping. | No estoy durmiendo. |
| You are not listening. | No estás escuchando. |
| He is not studying. | Él no está estudiando. |
| They are not working. | Ellos no están trabajando. |

### Contractions

| Full Form | Short Form |
|------------|------------|
| I am not | I'm not |
| You are not | You're not / You aren't |
| He is not | He's not / He isn't |
| She is not | She's not / She isn't |
| We are not | We're not / We aren't |
| They are not | They're not / They aren't |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Verb To Be + Subject + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Are you studying? | ¿Estás estudiando? |
| Is he working? | ¿Está trabajando? |
| Is she cooking? | ¿Está cocinando? |
| Are they playing soccer? | ¿Están jugando fútbol? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I am. | Sí. |
| No, I'm not. | No. |
| Yes, he is. | Sí. |
| No, she isn't. | No. |
| Yes, they are. | Sí. |
| No, they aren't. | No. |

# 🔤 Forming the -ING Form

## Most Verbs

Add **-ing**

| Base Verb | -ing Form |
|------------|------------|
| work | working |
| read | reading |
| play | playing |
| study | studying |

---

## Verbs Ending in -e

Remove **-e** and add **-ing**

| Base Verb | -ing Form |
|------------|------------|
| make | making |
| write | writing |
| dance | dancing |
| drive | driving |

---

## Short Verbs (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant)

Double the last consonant and add **-ing**

| Base Verb | -ing Form |
|------------|------------|
| run | running |
| sit | sitting |
| swim | swimming |
| get | getting |

# 📅 Uses of the Present Continuous

## 1. Actions Happening Now / Acciones que ocurren ahora

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am eating lunch. | Estoy almorzando. |
| She is talking on the phone. | Ella está hablando por teléfono. |

---

## 2. Temporary Situations / Situaciones Temporales

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| He is living with his uncle. | Él está viviendo con su tío. |
| We are working on a new project. | Estamos trabajando en un nuevo proyecto. |

---

## 3. Changing Situations / Situaciones Cambiantes

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| The weather is getting colder. | El clima se está volviendo más frío. |

## 4. Future Arrangements / Planes Futuros

- currently
- today
- this week

- tonight

### Examples

- She is studying now.
- I am working at the moment.
- They are traveling this week.

---

# 🚫 Stative Verbs

Some verbs are usually **not** used in the Present Continuous.

Algunos verbos normalmente **no** se usan en Presente Continuo.

| Verb | Español |
|--------|----------|
| know | saber |
| like | gustar |
| love | amar |
| want | querer |
| believe | creer |
| understand | entender |
| remember | recordar |

### Correct Examples

- I know the answer.
- She likes pizza.
- They want a new car.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I studying English. | I am studying English. |
>| She is study now. | She is studying now. |
>| Are you studying English? | ✅ Correct |
>| He am working. | He is working. |
>| They is playing. | They are playing. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - The Present Continuous always needs the verb **to be**.
> - The main verb must end in **-ing**.
> - Each subject uses its correct form of **to be**.

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

## Main Uses

- Actions happening now
- Temporary situations
- Changing situations
- Future arrangements

## Basic Structures

### Affirmative

Subject + To Be + Verb-ing

### Negative

Subject + To Be + not + Verb-ing

### Question

To Be + Subject + Verb-ing?

## Signal Words

- now
- right now
- at the moment
- currently
- today
- this week

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Always use the verb **to be**.
> - The main verb ends in **-ing**.
> - Use it for actions happening now.
> - Use it for temporary situations.
> - Some verbs like **know**, **like**, and **want** are usually not used in the Present Continuous.
> - Do not confuse the Present Continuous with the Simple Present.
