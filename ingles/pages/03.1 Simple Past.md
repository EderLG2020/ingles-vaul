
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The Simple Past is used for completed actions that happened at a specific time in the past.
>
> **Español:** El Pasado Simple se usa para acciones terminadas que ocurrieron en un momento específico del pasado.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I visited my grandparents yesterday.
> - She studied English last night.
> - They played soccer last weekend.
> - We traveled to Cusco in 2024.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + Past Verb + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I worked yesterday. | Trabajé ayer. |
| She visited her aunt. | Ella visitó a su tía. |
| They played soccer. | Ellos jugaron fútbol. |
| We watched a movie. | Nosotros vimos una película. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + did not (didn't) + Base Verb**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I didn't work yesterday. | No trabajé ayer. |
| She didn't call me. | Ella no me llamó. |
| They didn't play soccer. | Ellos no jugaron fútbol. |
| We didn't travel last year. | No viajamos el año pasado. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Did + Subject + Base Verb?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Did you study? | ¿Estudiaste? |
| Did she arrive on time? | ¿Ella llegó a tiempo? |
| Did they play well? | ¿Ellos jugaron bien? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I did. | Sí. |
| No, I didn't. | No. |
| Yes, she did. | Sí. |
| No, they didn't. | No. |

# 📖 Regular Verbs

Most regular verbs add **-ed**.

La mayoría de los verbos regulares agregan **-ed**.

| Base Verb | Past |
|------------|--------|
| work | worked |
| play | played |
| watch | watched |
| clean | cleaned |
| visit | visited |

### Examples

- I worked all day.
- She watched TV.
- They visited Lima.

---

# 🔄 Common Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs do not follow the **-ed** rule.

Los verbos irregulares no siguen la regla de **-ed**.

| Base Verb | Past |
|------------|--------|
| be | was / were |
| go | went |
| see | saw |
| eat | ate |
| have | had |
| do | did |
| come | came |
| write | wrote |
| speak | spoke |
| take | took |

### Examples

- I went to school.
- She ate breakfast.
- They wrote a letter.

# 📅 Uses of the Simple Past

## 1. Completed Actions

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I finished my homework. | Terminé mi tarea. |
| She cleaned her room. | Ella limpió su habitación. |

---

## 2. Past Habits

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| We played outside every day. | Jugábamos afuera todos los días. |
| He walked to school. | Él caminaba a la escuela. |

---

## 3. Past Events

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| They arrived late. | Ellos llegaron tarde. |
| The meeting started at 9 a.m. | La reunión comenzó a las 9 a.m. |

---

## 4. Series of Completed Actions

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I woke up, had breakfast, and went to work. | Me desperté, desayuné y fui al trabajo. |
| She opened the door and entered the house. | Ella abrió la puerta y entró a la casa. |

# ⚖️ Simple Past vs Present Perfect

| Simple Past | Present Perfect |
|--------------|----------------|
| I visited Spain in 2022. | I have visited Spain. |
| She finished yesterday. | She has finished. |
| Time is specific. | Time is not specific. |

# 📍 Using DID

Remember:

After **did** or **didn't**, the main verb returns to its base form.

Recuerda:

Después de **did** o **didn't**, el verbo principal vuelve a su forma base.

### Examples

- Did you go?
- She didn't study.
- They didn't eat lunch.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I didn't went. | I didn't go. |
>| Did she studied? | Did she study? |
>| They didn't ate. | They didn't eat. |
>| He go to school yesterday. | He went to school yesterday. |
>| We was happy. | We were happy. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - After **did** and **didn't**, always use the base form of the verb.
> - Irregular verbs have special past forms.
> - Use **was** with I, he, she, it and **were** with you, we, they.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

James: Hi, Olivia! How was your weekend?

Olivia: It was great! I visited my grandparents.

James: Nice! What did you do there?

Olivia: We cooked together, watched an old movie, and talked for hours.

James: That sounds fun. Did you stay there all weekend?

Olivia: Yes, I did. I came back on Sunday evening.

James: I stayed home. I finished a book and played some video games.

Olivia: That sounds relaxing too!

---

## Español

James: ¡Hola, Olivia! ¿Cómo estuvo tu fin de semana?

Olivia: ¡Fue genial! Visité a mis abuelos.

James: ¡Qué bien! ¿Qué hiciste allá?

Olivia: Cocinamos juntos, vimos una película antigua y conversamos durante horas.

James: Suena divertido. ¿Te quedaste todo el fin de semana?

Olivia: Sí. Regresé el domingo por la noche.

James: Yo me quedé en casa. Terminé un libro y jugué algunos videojuegos.

Olivia: ¡Eso también suena relajante!
