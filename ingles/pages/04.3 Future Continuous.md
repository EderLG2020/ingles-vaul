
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The Future Continuous is used for actions that **will be in progress at a specific time in the future**.
>
> **Español:** El Futuro Continuo se usa para acciones que **estarán en progreso en un momento específico del futuro**.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I will be studying at 8 p.m.
> - She will be working tomorrow morning.
> - They will be traveling next week.
> - We will be waiting for you.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + will + be + Verb-ing + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I will be studying. | Estaré estudiando. |
| She will be working. | Ella estará trabajando. |
| They will be playing soccer. | Ellos estarán jugando fútbol. |
| We will be traveling tomorrow. | Estaremos viajando mañana. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + will not + be + Verb-ing**

### Contraction

**will not = won't**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I won't be sleeping. | No estaré durmiendo. |
| She won't be coming. | Ella no estará viniendo. |
| They will not be waiting. | Ellos no estarán esperando. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Will + Subject + be + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Will you be studying? | ¿Estarás estudiando? |
| Will she be working tomorrow? | ¿Ella estará trabajando mañana? |
| Will they be traveling next month? | ¿Ellos estarán viajando el próximo mes? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I will. | Sí. |
| No, I won't. | No. |
| Yes, she will. | Sí. |
| No, they won't. | No. |

# 📅 Uses of the Future Continuous

## 1. Actions in Progress at a Future Time

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I will be studying at 9 p.m. | Estaré estudiando a las 9 p.m. |
| They will be working tomorrow morning. | Estarán trabajando mañana por la mañana. |

---

## 2. Future Actions That Will Naturally Happen

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| She will be driving to work at that time. | Ella estará conduciendo al trabajo a esa hora. |
| We will be flying over the ocean tomorrow. | Estaremos volando sobre el océano mañana. |

---

## 3. Asking Politely About Future Plans

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Will you be using your laptop tonight? | ¿Estarás usando tu laptop esta noche? |
| Will they be staying at the hotel? | ¿Se estarán quedando en el hotel? |

---

## 4. Two Actions in the Future

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| While you are sleeping, I will be working. | Mientras estés durmiendo, yo estaré trabajando. |
| When you arrive, we will be having dinner. | Cuando llegues, estaremos cenando. |

---

# 🔑 Signal Words / Palabras Clave

Common expressions used with the Future Continuous:

- at this time tomorrow
- tomorrow morning
- tomorrow afternoon
- tomorrow night
- next week
- next month
- at 8 p.m.
- when
- while

### Examples

- I will be studying at this time tomorrow.
- She will be traveling next week.
- We will be eating when you arrive.

---

# ⚖️ Future Continuous vs Will

| Future Continuous | Will |
|-------------------|------|
| Action in progress in the future | Simple future action |
| I will be studying at 8 p.m. | I will study tonight. |
| Focus on the process | Focus on the action |

---

# ⚖️ Future Continuous vs Going To

| Future Continuous | Going To |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Future action in progress | Future plan or intention |
| I will be working tomorrow. | I am going to work tomorrow. |

---

# 📍 Future Continuous with When and While

## When

Introduces another future action.

### Examples

- I will be sleeping when you call.
- They will be eating when we arrive.

---

## While

Shows two actions happening at the same time.

### Examples

- I will be studying while my sister is watching TV.
- We will be traveling while you are working.

---

# 📍 Future Continuous with Stative Verbs

Stative verbs are generally **not used** in continuous forms.

Los verbos de estado generalmente **no se usan** en tiempos continuos.

### Correct

- I will know the answer.
- She will understand the lesson.

### Incorrect

- I will be knowing the answer.
- She will be understanding the lesson.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I will be study. | I will be studying. |
>| She will studying. | She will be studying. |
>| Will you be work tomorrow? | Will you be working tomorrow? |
>| They won't be play soccer. | They won't be playing soccer. |
>| He will be knowing the answer. | He will know the answer. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - The structure is always **will + be + verb-ing**.
> - The main verb must end in **-ing**.
> - Most stative verbs are not used in continuous tenses.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

Chloe: Will you be free in the evening?

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

## Main Uses

- Actions in progress at a future time
- Future events that will naturally happen
- Polite questions about future plans
- Simultaneous future actions

## Basic Structures

### Affirmative

Subject + will + be + Verb-ing

### Negative

Subject + will not + be + Verb-ing

### Question

Will + Subject + be + Verb-ing?

## Signal Words

- at this time tomorrow
- tomorrow morning
- next week
- when
- while

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Use **will + be + verb-ing**.
> - Focus on actions that will be in progress.
> - Use **when** and **while** to connect future actions.
> - Do not use most stative verbs in continuous forms.
> - After **will be**, always use the **-ing** form of the verb.
> - The Future Continuous emphasizes the process, not just the future action.
