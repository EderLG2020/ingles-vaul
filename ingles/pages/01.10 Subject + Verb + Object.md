> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The basic English sentence order is **Subject + Verb + Object (SVO)**. This is the most common structure in everyday communication.
>
> **Español:** El orden básico de una oración en inglés es **Sujeto + Verbo + Objeto (SVO)**. Es la estructura más común en la comunicación diaria.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I eat apples.
> - She reads books.
> - They play soccer.

# 🧩 Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + Verb + Object**

**Sujeto + Verbo + Objeto**

|---|---|

# 👤 Subject / Sujeto

The subject tells us **who or what performs the action**.

El sujeto nos dice **quién o qué realiza la acción**.

|Subject|Español|
|---|---|
|I|Yo|
|You|Tú / Usted|
|He|Él|
|She|Ella|
|It|Eso / Ello|
|We|Nosotros|
|They|Ellos|

### Examples

- I work.
    
- She sings.
    
- They study.
    

---

# ⚙️ Verb / Verbo

The verb describes **the action or state**.

El verbo describe **la acción o el estado**.

|Verb|Español|
|---|---|
|eat|comer|
|drink|beber|
|read|leer|
|write|escribir|
|play|jugar|
|watch|ver|
|love|amar|

### Examples

- I read.
    
- She plays.
    
- They watch.

# 📦 Object / Objeto

El objeto recibe la acción.

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes

|Incorrect ❌|Correct ✅|
|---|---|
|Eat I apples.|I eat apples.|
|She a book reads.|She reads a book.|
|Play they soccer.|They play soccer.|
|I coffee drink.|I drink coffee.|

## Why? / ¿Por qué?

|English|Español|
|---|---|
|English follows a fixed Subject + Verb + Object order.|El inglés sigue un orden fijo de Sujeto + Verbo + Objeto.|
|Changing the order can make the sentence incorrect.|Cambiar el orden puede hacer que la oración sea incorrecta.|

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

## Basic Formula

**Subject + Verb + Object**

## Essential Examples

- I eat apples.
    
- She reads books.
    
- We study English.
    
- They play soccer.
    

## Golden Rules ⭐

- Every sentence usually starts with a subject.
    
- The verb comes after the subject.
    
- The object receives the action.
    
- English word order is usually fixed.
    
- **Subject + Verb + Object** is the foundation of English grammar.
