
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** We use **will** to talk about future predictions, spontaneous decisions, promises, offers, and facts about the future.
>
> **Español:** Usamos **will** para hablar de predicciones, decisiones espontáneas, promesas, ofrecimientos y hechos futuros.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I will help you.
> - She will arrive tomorrow.
> - It will rain today.
> - We will never forget this day.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + will + Base Verb + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I will study tonight. | Estudiaré esta noche. |
| She will call you. | Ella te llamará. |
| They will travel next month. | Ellos viajarán el próximo mes. |
| We will finish the project. | Terminaremos el proyecto. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + will not + Base Verb**

### Contraction

**will not = won't**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I won't forget. | No olvidaré. |
| She won't come today. | Ella no vendrá hoy. |
| They will not agree. | Ellos no estarán de acuerdo. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Will + Subject + Base Verb?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Will you help me? | ¿Me ayudarás? |
| Will she arrive soon? | ¿Llegará pronto? |
| Will they join us? | ¿Se unirán a nosotros? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I will. | Sí. |
| No, I won't. | No. |
| Yes, she will. | Sí. |
| No, they won't. | No. |

## 1. Predictions

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|

Decisiones tomadas en el momento de hablar.

# 🔑 Signal Words / Palabras Clave

Common expressions used with **will**:

- tomorrow
- tonight
- next week
- next month
- next year
- soon
- later
- in the future
- one day

### Examples

- I will visit my family tomorrow.
- She will start her new job next week.
- One day, we will travel around the world.

---

# ⚖️ Will vs Going To

| Will | Going To |
|-------|-----------|
| Spontaneous decision | Planned intention |
| Prediction | Prediction with evidence |
| Promise | Plan |
| I will answer the phone. | I am going to study tonight. |

### Examples

- I will help you.
- I am going to visit my grandparents this weekend.

---

# 📍 Contractions

| Full Form | Short Form |
|------------|------------|
| I will | I'll |
| You will | You'll |
| He will | He'll |
| She will | She'll |
| We will | We'll |
| They will | They'll |
| Will not | Won't |

### Examples

- I'll call you later.
- She'll arrive soon.
- They won't be late.

---

# 📍 Will Be

**Will be** is used to talk about future states or situations.

### Examples

- I will be happy.
- She will be at home.
- They will be ready tomorrow.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I will to study. | I will study. |
>| She will goes. | She will go. |
>| Will you to help me? | Will you help me? |
>| I won't to forget. | I won't forget. |
>| He wills arrive tomorrow. | He will arrive tomorrow. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - After **will**, always use the base form of the verb.
> - Never add **to** after **will**.
> - The verb never changes after **will**.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Kevin: What are your plans for tomorrow?

Anna: I'm not sure yet.

Kevin: I will go to the library. Do you want to come?

Anna: That sounds good. I think I will join you.

Kevin: Great! We will study together.

Anna: Thanks! I will bring my notes.

Kevin: Perfect. After that, we will have lunch.

Anna: Good idea. I won't forget to bring my notebook.

Kevin: Excellent. I know we will have a productive day.

---

## Español

Kevin: ¿Cuáles son tus planes para mañana?

Anna: Todavía no estoy segura.

Kevin: Iré a la biblioteca. ¿Quieres venir?

Anna: Suena bien. Creo que iré contigo.

Kevin: ¡Genial! Estudiaremos juntos.

Anna: ¡Gracias! Llevaré mis apuntes.

Kevin: Perfecto. Después almorzaremos.

Anna: Buena idea. No olvidaré llevar mi cuaderno.

Kevin: Excelente. Sé que tendremos un día productivo.
