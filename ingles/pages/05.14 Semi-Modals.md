

> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** **Semi-modals** are verbs that behave partly like modal verbs and partly like regular verbs. They express **obligation, necessity, advice, habit, and courage**.
>
> **Español:** Los **semi-modales** son verbos que se comportan en parte como verbos modales y en parte como verbos regulares. Expresan **obligación, necesidad, consejo, hábito y valentía**.
>
> **Common Semi-Modals:**
>
> - Have to
> - Need to
> - Ought to
> - Used to
> - Dare
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I have to work.
> - You need to study.
> - We ought to help.
> - She used to live here.
> - He doesn't dare to ask.

# 📚 What Are Semi-Modals?

Semi-modals are different from pure modal verbs because they can change according to the subject and tense.

Los semi-modales son diferentes de los verbos modales puros porque pueden cambiar según el sujeto y el tiempo verbal.

### Examples

| Modal | Semi-Modal |
|---------|-------------|
| can | have to |
| must | need to |
| may | ought to |
| will | used to |
| would | dare |

---

# 📅 Common Semi-Modals

## 1. Have To

### Use

External obligation or necessity.

Obligación o necesidad externa.

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I have to work tomorrow. | Tengo que trabajar mañana. |
| She has to wake up early. | Ella tiene que levantarse temprano. |

---

## 2. Need To

### Use

Necessity or requirement.

Necesidad o requisito.

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| You need to study. | Necesitas estudiar. |
| They need to leave now. | Necesitan irse ahora. |

---

## 3. Ought To

### Use

Advice and moral obligation.

Consejo y obligación moral.

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| You ought to apologize. | Deberías disculparte. |
| We ought to protect nature. | Deberíamos proteger la naturaleza. |

---

## 4. Used To

### Use

Past habits and past situations that no longer exist.

Hábitos pasados y situaciones que ya no existen.

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I used to play soccer. | Solía jugar fútbol. |
| She used to live in Lima. | Ella solía vivir en Lima. |

---

## 5. Dare

### Use

Courage or challenge.

Valentía o desafío.

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I don't dare to ask. | No me atrevo a preguntar. |
| How dare you say that! | ¡Cómo te atreves a decir eso! |

# 📍 Characteristics of Semi-Modals

## 1. They Can Change Form

| Subject | Example |
|----------|----------|
| I | have to |
| He | has to |
| She | needs to |
| They | used to |

---

## 2. They Use Auxiliary Verbs

Questions and negatives often require **do/does/did**.

Las preguntas y negaciones suelen requerir **do/does/did**.

### Examples

- Do you have to work?
- She doesn't need to hurry.
- Did you use to live here?

---

## 3. Some Use "To"

Unlike most modal verbs, many semi-modals are followed by **to**.

A diferencia de la mayoría de los verbos modales, muchos semi-modales van seguidos de **to**.

### Examples

- have to work
- need to study
- ought to help
- dare to ask

---

# ⚖️ Modal Verbs vs Semi-Modals

| Feature | Modal Verbs | Semi-Modals |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Change with subject | No | Yes |
| Use do/does in questions | No | Usually yes |
| Use "to" | No | Often yes |
| Have different tenses | Limited | Yes |

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| She have to work. | She has to work. |
>| I don't need study. | I don't need to study. |
>| You ought study more. | You ought to study more. |
>| Did you used to play? | Did you use to play? |
>| I don't dare ask him. | I don't dare to ask him. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - Semi-modals can change according to the subject.
> - Most semi-modals require **to**.
> - **Used to** loses the **-d** after **did**.
> - Questions and negatives often use auxiliary verbs.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Mia: You look worried.

Ethan: I have to finish a big project.

Mia: You also need to get some rest.

Ethan: I know. I ought to manage my time better.

Mia: Did you use to work this much?

Ethan: No, I used to have more free time.

Mia: Then you shouldn't worry too much.

Ethan: You're right. I don't dare to say no to extra work.

Mia: Sometimes you have to think about your health too.

---

## Español

Mia: Te ves preocupado.

Ethan: Tengo que terminar un gran proyecto.

Mia: También necesitas descansar un poco.

Ethan: Lo sé. Debería organizar mejor mi tiempo.

Mia: ¿Solías trabajar tanto?

Ethan: No, antes tenía más tiempo libre.

Mia: Entonces no deberías preocuparte tanto.

Ethan: Tienes razón. No me atrevo a decir que no al trabajo extra.

Mia: A veces también tienes que pensar en tu salud.
