
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The first impression matters. Use greetings and introductions to start conversations naturally.
>
> **Español:** La primera impresión importa. Usa saludos y presentaciones para iniciar conversaciones de forma natural.
>
> Examples:
>
> - Hello, my name is John.
> - Nice to meet you.
> - How are you?

# 💬 Small Introduction Template

### Basic Version

**English**

Hello! My name is Alex. I am twenty years old. I am from Peru. I am a student. Nice to meet you.

**Español**

¡Hola! Mi nombre es Alex. Tengo veinte años. Soy de Perú. Soy estudiante. Mucho gusto.

---

### Intermediate Version

**English**

Hello, my name is Alex. I'm from Peru and I work as a software developer. I enjoy learning English and meeting new people.

**Español**

Hola, mi nombre es Alex. Soy de Perú y trabajo como desarrollador de software. Disfruto aprender inglés y conocer nuevas personas.

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes

| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
|-------------|------------|
| I am Carlos name. | My name is Carlos. |
| I have 20 years. | I am 20 years old. |
| Nice meet you. | Nice to meet you. |
| I from Peru. | I am from Peru. |

---

## Why? / ¿Por qué?

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| English requires the verb "to be". | El inglés requiere el verbo "to be". |
| Age is expressed with "I am", not "I have". | La edad se expresa con "I am", no con "I have". |

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

### Essential Greetings

- Hello
- Hi
- Good morning
- Good afternoon
- Good evening

### Essential Introductions

- My name is...
- I'm...
- I am from...
- Nice to meet you.

### Golden Rules ⭐

1. Use greetings before starting a conversation.
2. Introduce yourself with "My name is..." or "I'm...".
3. Use "I am" for age, nationality, and occupation.
4. Always respond politely to introductions.
5. "Nice to meet you" is one of the most important expressions in English.
