

> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** Numbers are used for counting, age, prices, dates, time, percentages, and large quantities.
>
> **Español:** Los números se utilizan para contar, expresar edad, precios, fechas, horas, porcentajes y cantidades grandes.
>
> Examples:
>
> - I have **three** books.
> - She is **twenty** years old.
> - The company made **one million dollars**.
> - The success rate is **95 percent**.

> [!warning] False Friend Alert
>
> **English:** One billion = 1,000,000,000
>
> **Español:** One billion = Mil millones
>
> ❌ Billion = Billón
>
> ✅ Billion = Mil millones

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

### Key Numbers

| Number | English |
|----------|----------|
| 100 | One hundred |
| 1,000 | One thousand |
| 1,000,000 | One million |
| 1,000,000,000 | One billion |
| 1,000,000,000,000 | One trillion |

### Golden Rules ⭐

1. Use hyphens between 21–99.
   - Twenty-one
   - Forty-five

2. Use "point" for decimals.
   - 3.14 → Three point one four

3. Phone numbers are read digit by digit.

4. One billion = Mil millones.

5. Learn numbers up to at least one trillion.
