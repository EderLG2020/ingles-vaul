

> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** Both **used to** and **would** can describe repeated actions in the past. However, **used to** can also describe past states or situations, while **would cannot**.
>
> **Español:** Tanto **used to** como **would** pueden describir acciones repetidas en el pasado. Sin embargo, **used to** también puede describir estados o situaciones pasadas, mientras que **would no puede**.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I used to play soccer every weekend.
> - My grandfather would tell us stories.
> - She used to live in Lima.
> - We would go fishing every summer.

# 📚 What Is "Used To"? / ¿Qué Es "Used To"?

**Used to** describes:

- Past habits
- Repeated actions
- Past situations or states that are no longer true

**Used to** describe:

- Hábitos pasados
- Acciones repetidas
- Situaciones o estados pasados que ya no son ciertos

---

## Structure

### Affirmative

**Subject + used to + Base Verb**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I used to play soccer. | Solía jugar fútbol. |
| She used to live in Peru. | Ella solía vivir en Perú. |
| They used to visit us every year. | Ellos solían visitarnos cada año. |

---

## Negative

**Subject + didn't use to + Base Verb**

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I didn't use to drink coffee. | No solía tomar café. |
| She didn't use to like math. | Ella no solía gustarle matemáticas. |

---

## Questions

**Did + Subject + use to + Base Verb?**

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Did you use to play tennis? | ¿Solías jugar tenis? |
| Did she use to live here? | ¿Solía vivir aquí? |

---

# 📚 What Is "Would"? / ¿Qué Es "Would"?

**Would** describes repeated actions in the past.

**Would** describe acciones repetidas en el pasado.

It is often used when telling stories or remembering childhood.

A menudo se usa al contar historias o recordar la infancia.

---

## Structure

### Affirmative

**Subject + would + Base Verb**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| My father would read stories every night. | Mi padre solía leer cuentos todas las noches. |
| We would swim in the river during summer. | Solíamos nadar en el río durante el verano. |
| They would visit their grandparents every Sunday. | Solían visitar a sus abuelos todos los domingos. |

---

# ⚖️ Used To vs Would

| Used To | Would |
|-----------|--------|
| Past habits | Past habits |
| Past states | ❌ Not used for states |
| Repeated actions | Repeated actions |
| Common in everyday English | Common in storytelling |

---

## Both Are Correct

| English |
|-----------|
| I used to play outside every day. |
| I would play outside every day. |

---

## Only "Used To" Is Correct

| Correct ✅ | Incorrect ❌ |
|------------|--------------|
| I used to live in Cusco. | I would live in Cusco. |
| She used to have long hair. | She would have long hair. |
| They used to know my family. | They would know my family. |

# 📅 Common Uses

## 1. Past Habits

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I used to wake up early. | Solía despertarme temprano. |
| I would wake up early every morning. | Solía despertarme temprano todas las mañanas. |

---

## 2. Childhood Memories

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| My grandmother would cook delicious meals. | Mi abuela solía cocinar comidas deliciosas. |
| We would play in the park after school. | Solíamos jugar en el parque después de clases. |

---

## 3. Past States

(Only **used to**)

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I used to be shy. | Solía ser tímido. |
| She used to work here. | Ella solía trabajar aquí. |
| They used to own a small shop. | Ellos solían tener una pequeña tienda. |

---

# 🔑 Signal Words / Palabras Clave

Common expressions used with **used to** and **would**:

- when I was a child
- every day
- every weekend
- every summer
- in the past
- years ago
- when we were young

### Examples

- When I was a child, I used to ride my bike every day.
- Every summer, we would travel to the beach.

---

# 📍 Used To vs Be Used To

These expressions have different meanings.

Estas expresiones tienen significados diferentes.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|----------|
| used to | past habit |
| be used to | be accustomed to |

### Examples

- I used to wake up early.
- I am used to waking up early.

---

# 📍 Used To vs Get Used To

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|----------|
| used to | past habit |
| get used to | become accustomed to |

### Examples

- She used to work at home.
- She is getting used to working at home.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I would live in Lima. | I used to live in Lima. |
>| Did you used to play soccer? | Did you use to play soccer? |
>| I didn't used to drink coffee. | I didn't use to drink coffee. |
>| She would have a red car. | She used to have a red car. |
>| They used to going there. | They used to go there. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - **Would** cannot describe past states.
> - After **did** or **didn't**, use **use to**, not **used to**.
> - After **used to**, use the base form of the verb.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Emma: What did you do when you were a child?

Lucas: I used to play soccer with my friends every afternoon.

Emma: Really? I would ride my bicycle around the neighborhood.

Lucas: My grandfather would also take me fishing every summer.

Emma: That sounds fun. Did you use to live in the countryside?

Lucas: Yes, I did. We used to have a small farm.

Emma: I used to live in the city, so my weekends were very different.

Lucas: It's interesting how our childhoods were different.

Emma: Yes, but we both have wonderful memories.

---

## Español

Emma: ¿Qué hacías cuando eras niño?

Lucas: Solía jugar fútbol con mis amigos todas las tardes.

Emma: ¿En serio? Yo solía andar en bicicleta por el vecindario.

Lucas: Mi abuelo también solía llevarme a pescar cada verano.

Emma: Suena divertido. ¿Solías vivir en el campo?

Lucas: Sí. Solíamos tener una pequeña granja.

Emma: Yo solía vivir en la ciudad, así que mis fines de semana eran muy diferentes.

Lucas: Es interesante cómo nuestras infancias fueron distintas.

Emma: Sí, pero ambos tenemos recuerdos maravillosos.

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

## Use "Used To" For

- Past habits
- Past routines
- Past states
- Situations that are no longer true

## Use "Would" For

- Repeated past actions
- Memories
- Stories
- Habits

## Remember

| Used To | Would |
|-----------|--------|
| Habits ✅ | Habits ✅ |
| States ✅ | States ❌ |

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Use **used to** for habits and past states.
> - Use **would** only for repeated past actions.
> - After **did** and **didn't**, write **use to**.
> - After **used to**, use the base form of the verb.
> - Do not use **would** for possession, feelings, or permanent situations.
> - **Used to**, **be used to**, and **get used to** have different meanings.
