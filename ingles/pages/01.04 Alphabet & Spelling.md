

> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** English spelling and pronunciation are not always the same.
>
> **Español:** La escritura y la pronunciación en inglés no siempre coinciden.
>
> Examples:
>
> - Name → /neɪm/
> - Phone → /foʊn/
> - Enough → /ɪˈnʌf/
>
> Learn both the spelling and the pronunciation.

# 📖 Basic Spelling Rules

## Rule 1: I before E

Usually:

> I before E, except after C.

Examples:

- Believe
- Field

Exceptions:

- Weird
- Science

---

## Rule 2: Silent E

The final "e" is often silent.

Examples:

| Word | Pronunciation |
|----------|----------|
| Name | neim |
| Make | meik |
| Home | houm |

---

## Rule 3: Double Consonants

Examples:

- Running
- Swimming
- Stopping

---

## Rule 4: C and G Sounds

### Soft C

Before E, I, Y

Examples:

- City
- Center
- Cycle

### Hard C

Before A, O, U

Examples:

- Cat
- Coffee
- Cup
