
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
> 
> **English:** Every sentence needs a subject.
> 
> **Español:** Toda oración necesita un sujeto.
> 
> The subject tells us **who** does the action.
> 
> El sujeto nos dice **quién** realiza la acción.
> 
> Examples:
> 
> - I am happy.
>     
> - She is a teacher.
>     
> - They are students.
>

## 📖 Theory / Teoría

### What is a Personal Pronoun? / ¿Qué es un Pronombre Personal?

|English|Español|
|---|---|
|A personal pronoun replaces a person, animal, place, or thing.|Un pronombre personal reemplaza a una persona, animal, lugar o cosa.|

### Subject Pronouns / Pronombres Sujeto

|Pronoun|Spanish|Example|
|---|---|---|
|I|Yo|I am happy.|
|You|Tú / Usted|You are here.|
|He|Él|He is tall.|
|She|Ella|She is my friend.|
|It|Eso / Ello|It is new.|
|We|Nosotros|We are students.|
|They|Ellos / Ellas|They are busy.|

## 🧠 Special Notes / Notas Especiales

### You

|English|Español|
|---|---|
|"You" is used for one person and for multiple people.|"You" se usa tanto para una persona como para varias personas.|

Examples:

- You are my friend. → Tú eres mi amigo.
    
- You are my friends. → Ustedes son mis amigos.
    

---

### It

|English|Español|
|---|---|
|Use "it" for animals, objects, ideas, weather, time, and distances.|Usa "it" para animales, objetos, ideas, el clima, la hora y las distancias.|

Examples:

- It is raining.
    
- It is cold today.
    
- It is a beautiful car.
    

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes

|Incorrect ❌|Correct ✅|
|---|---|
|Is happy.|She is happy.|
|Am tired.|I am tired.|
|My friend is tall. He tall.|My friend is tall. He is tall.|

### Why? / ¿Por qué?

|English|Español|
|---|---|
|Every sentence needs a subject and a verb.|Toda oración necesita un sujeto y un verbo.|

## 📝 Practice / Práctica

Choose the correct pronoun.

1. Maria is happy. → _____ is happy.
    
2. John and Mike are friends. → _____ are friends.
    
3. My dog is small. → _____ is small.
    
4. Ana and I are students. → _____ are students.
    
5. Peter is a doctor. → _____ is a doctor.
    

### Answers / Respuestas

1. She
    
2. They
    
3. It
    
4. We
    
5. He
