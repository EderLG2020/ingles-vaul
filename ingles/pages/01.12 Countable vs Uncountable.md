
> Regla rápida:
>
> **Countable** → se puede contar (1, 2, 3...)
>
> **Uncountable** → no se cuenta directamente

---

| Tipo | ¿Se cuenta? | Singular | Plural | Ejemplos |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Countable ✅ | Sí | Sí | Sí | apple, book, car |
| Uncountable ❌ | No | Sí | No | water, money, information |

---

# Countable (Contables)

| Correcto ✅ | Incorrecto ❌ |
|---|---|
| one apple | one apples |
| two apples | two apple |
| three books | three book |
| five cars | five car |

Ejemplos:

apple → apples  
book → books  
student → students  

---

# Uncountable (No contables)

| Correcto ✅ | Incorrecto ❌ |
|---|---|
| water | waters |
| information | informations |
| money | moneys |
| bread | breads |

Ejemplos:

I need water. ✅  
I need waters. ❌

She gave me information. ✅  
She gave me informations. ❌

---

# ¿Cómo contar algo incontable?

Usa una unidad.

| Uncountable | Forma correcta |
|---|---|
| water | a bottle of water |
| bread | a piece of bread |
| information | a piece of information |
| rice | a bowl of rice |
| furniture | a piece of furniture |

Ejemplos:

✅ two bottles of water  
✅ three pieces of information  
✅ five pieces of bread  

---

# Mini resumen

| Si puedes decir... | Entonces |
|---|---|
| 1 apple → 2 apples | Countable ✅ |
| 1 water → 2 waters | Uncountable ❌ |
