
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** **Could** is a modal verb used to express **past ability, polite requests, permission, possibility, and suggestions**.
>
> **Español:** **Could** es un verbo modal que se usa para expresar **habilidad en el pasado, solicitudes corteses, permiso, posibilidad y sugerencias**.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I could swim when I was five.
> - Could you help me?
> - It could rain later.
> - We could go to the park.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + could + Base Verb + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I could swim when I was young. | Podía nadar cuando era joven. |
| She could play the piano. | Ella podía tocar el piano. |
| They could solve difficult problems. | Ellos podían resolver problemas difíciles. |
| We could see the mountains. | Podíamos ver las montañas. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + could not + Base Verb**

### Contraction

**could not = couldn't**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I couldn't sleep last night. | No pude dormir anoche. |
| She could not attend the meeting. | Ella no pudo asistir a la reunión. |
| They couldn't find the keys. | Ellos no pudieron encontrar las llaves. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Could + Subject + Base Verb?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Could you help me? | ¿Podrías ayudarme? |
| Could she drive when she was eighteen? | ¿Podía conducir cuando tenía dieciocho años? |
| Could they come tomorrow? | ¿Podrían venir mañana? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I could. | Sí. |
| No, I couldn't. | No. |
| Yes, she could. | Sí. |
| No, they couldn't. | No. |

# 📅 Uses of Could

## 1. Past Ability

Talking about abilities in the past.

Hablar sobre habilidades en el pasado.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I could ride a bike when I was six. | Podía montar bicicleta cuando tenía seis años. |
| She could read before she started school. | Ella podía leer antes de empezar la escuela. |

---

## 2. Polite Requests

More polite than **can**.

Más cortés que **can**.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Could you open the window? | ¿Podrías abrir la ventana? |
| Could you repeat that, please? | ¿Podrías repetir eso, por favor? |

---

## 3. Permission

Formal or polite permission.

Permiso formal o cortés.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Could I ask a question? | ¿Podría hacer una pregunta? |
| Could we leave early today? | ¿Podríamos salir temprano hoy? |

---

## 4. Possibility

Talking about something that may happen.

Hablar sobre algo que puede suceder.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| It could rain this afternoon. | Podría llover esta tarde. |
| This could be the answer. | Esta podría ser la respuesta. |

---

## 5. Suggestions

Making suggestions.

Hacer sugerencias.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| We could watch a movie. | Podríamos ver una película. |
| You could talk to your teacher. | Podrías hablar con tu profesor. |

---

# 🔑 Common Expressions with Could

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Could you...? | ¿Podrías...? |
| Could I...? | ¿Podría...? |
| We could... | Podríamos... |
| It could be... | Podría ser... |
| You could try... | Podrías intentar... |

---

# ⚖️ Could vs Can

| Could | Can |
|--------|-----|
| Past ability | Present ability |
| Polite request | Informal request |
| Less certain possibility | General possibility |
| I could swim at six. | I can swim now. |
| Could you help me? | Can you help me? |

---

# ⚖️ Could vs Might

| Could | Might |
|--------|--------|
| Possible situation | Less certain possibility |
| It could rain. | It might rain. |
| We could win. | We might win. |

---

# 📍 Rules for Modal Verbs

After **could**, always use the base form of the verb.

Después de **could**, siempre se usa el verbo en su forma base.

### Correct

- I could swim.
- She could help.
- They could study.

### Incorrect

- I could to swim.
- She could helps.
- They could studying.

---

# 📍 Couldn't

**Couldn't** is the contraction of **could not**.

### Examples

- I couldn't understand the lesson.
- She couldn't come yesterday.
- They couldn't finish the project.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I could to swim. | I could swim. |
>| She could drives. | She could drive. |
>| Could you to help me? | Could you help me? |
>| They couldn't playing soccer. | They couldn't play soccer. |
>| He coulds speak English. | He could speak English. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - After **could**, always use the base form.
> - Never use **to** after **could**.
> - Modal verbs never take **-s**, **-ed**, or **-ing**.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Noah: Could you help me with my homework?

Grace: Of course. What do you need?

Noah: I couldn't understand today's math lesson.

Grace: Don't worry. We could study together.

Noah: Thanks! Could we meet after school?

Grace: Yes, we could meet at the library.

Noah: Great! My brother could help us too. He's very good at math.

Grace: Perfect. I think we could finish everything before dinner.

## Español

Noah: ¿Podrías ayudarme con mi tarea?

Grace: Claro. ¿Qué necesitas?

Noah: No pude entender la clase de matemáticas de hoy.

Grace: No te preocupes. Podríamos estudiar juntos.

Noah: ¡Gracias! ¿Podríamos reunirnos después de clases?

Grace: Sí, podríamos encontrarnos en la biblioteca.

Noah: Genial. Mi hermano también podría ayudarnos. Es muy bueno en matemáticas.

Grace: Perfecto. Creo que podríamos terminar todo antes de la cena.

## Main Uses

- Past ability
- Polite requests
- Permission
- Possibility
- Suggestions

## Basic Structures

### Affirmative

Subject + could + Base Verb

### Negative

Subject + could not (couldn't) + Base Verb

### Question

Could + Subject + Base Verb?

- Could you...?
- Could I...?
- We could...
- It could be...

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Use **could** for past ability.
> - Use **could** for polite requests and permission.
> - Use **could** for possibilities and suggestions.
> - After **could**, always use the base form of the verb.
> - Never use **to** after **could**.
> - Modal verbs do not change according to the subject.
