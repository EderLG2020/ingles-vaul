
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** Every sentence needs a verb.
>
> **Español:** Toda oración necesita un verbo.
>
> ❌ I student.
>
> ✅ I am a student.
>
> ---
>
> **English:** Use **am / is / are** according to the subject.
>
> **Español:** Usa **am / is / are** según el sujeto.
>
> I → am
>
> He / She / It → is
>
> You / We / They → are

## 📖 Theory / Teoría

### Forms / Formas

| Subject | Verb | Traducción |
|----------|------|-------------|
| I | am | soy / estoy |
| You | are | eres / estás |
| He | is | él es / está |
| She | is | ella es / está |
| It | is | es / está |
| We | are | somos / estamos |
| They | are | son / están |

## ❌ Negative Form / Forma Negativa

### Rule / Regla

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Add **not** after the verb. | Agrega **not** después del verbo. |

| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| I am | I am not |
| You are | You are not / You aren't |
| He is | He is not / He isn't |
| We are | We are not / We aren't |

### Examples / Ejemplos

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am not tired. | No estoy cansado. |
| She isn't here. | Ella no está aquí. |
| They aren't ready. | Ellos no están listos. |

## ❓ Questions / Preguntas

> [!info] Rule / Regla
>
> **English:** Invert the verb and the subject.
>
> **Español:** Invierte el verbo y el sujeto.
