
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** The Present Continuous can be used to talk about **future arrangements and definite plans**, especially when the time and place are already decided.
>
> **Español:** El Presente Continuo puede usarse para hablar de **planes futuros y arreglos definidos**, especialmente cuando el tiempo y el lugar ya están decididos.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - I am meeting my friends tonight.
> - She is traveling next week.
> - We are having dinner with our grandparents tomorrow.
> - They are moving to a new apartment next month.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing + Future Time Expression**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am visiting my aunt tomorrow. | Voy a visitar a mi tía mañana. |
| She is meeting her teacher this afternoon. | Ella se reunirá con su profesora esta tarde. |
| They are traveling next month. | Ellos viajarán el próximo mes. |
| We are having lunch together tomorrow. | Almorzaremos juntos mañana. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + am/is/are + not + Verb-ing**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am not working tomorrow. | No trabajaré mañana. |
| She isn't coming tonight. | Ella no vendrá esta noche. |
| They aren't staying at the hotel. | Ellos no se quedarán en el hotel. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Are you meeting your friends? | ¿Te reunirás con tus amigos? |
| Is she coming to the party? | ¿Ella vendrá a la fiesta? |
| Are they traveling this weekend? | ¿Ellos viajarán este fin de semana? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I am. | Sí. |
| No, I'm not. | No. |
| Yes, she is. | Sí. |
| No, they aren't. | No. |

# 📅 Uses of the Present Continuous for Future

## 1. Personal Arrangements

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I am meeting my doctor tomorrow. | Me reuniré con mi médico mañana. |
| We are visiting our cousins this weekend. | Visitaremos a nuestros primos este fin de semana. |

---

## 2. Fixed Plans

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| She is starting a new job next month. | Ella comenzará un nuevo trabajo el próximo mes. |
| They are moving to another city. | Ellos se mudarán a otra ciudad. |

---

## 3. Events Already Organized

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| We are having a meeting at 10 a.m. | Tendremos una reunión a las 10 a.m. |

| They are leaving early in the morning. | Saldrán temprano por la mañana. |

# ⚖️ Present Continuous vs Going To

| Present Continuous | Going To |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Definite arrangement | Future intention or plan |
| I am meeting my boss tomorrow. | I am going to look for a new job. |
| Usually organized | May simply be an intention |

---

# ⚖️ Present Continuous vs Present Simple for Future

| Present Continuous | Present Simple |
|-------------------|----------------|
| Personal arrangement | Public schedule |
| I am meeting my teacher tomorrow. | The class starts at 8 a.m. |
| Personal plan | Official timetable |

# 📍 Present Continuous for Future vs Will

| Present Continuous | Will |
|-------------------|------|
| Planned arrangement | Spontaneous decision |
| I am seeing the dentist tomorrow. | I will answer the phone. |
| Organized event | Instant action |

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I am meet my friends tomorrow. | I am meeting my friends tomorrow. |
>| She is come tonight. | She is coming tonight. |
>| They are travel next week. | They are traveling next week. |
>| We meeting at 8 p.m. | We are meeting at 8 p.m. |
>| He is going party tomorrow. | He is going to the party tomorrow. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - Use **am / is / are + verb-ing**.
> - The main verb must end in **-ing**.
> - This structure is used for definite future arrangements.
> - Include a future time expression whenever possible.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Oliver: What are you doing this weekend?

Mia: I am visiting my grandparents.

Oliver: That sounds nice. Are you staying there all weekend?

Mia: Yes, I am staying until Sunday.

Oliver: I am meeting some friends on Saturday afternoon.

Mia: Really? Where are you meeting them?

Oliver: We are meeting at the new café near the park.

Mia: Great! I am coming back on Sunday, so maybe we can meet then.

Oliver: Perfect! We are having dinner at my house that night.

Mia: Wonderful! I am looking forward to it.

## Español

Oliver: ¿Qué harás este fin de semana?

Mia: Voy a visitar a mis abuelos.

Oliver: Suena bien. ¿Te quedarás todo el fin de semana?

Mia: Sí, me quedaré hasta el domingo.

Oliver: Yo me reuniré con unos amigos el sábado por la tarde.

Mia: ¿En serio? ¿Dónde se reunirán?

Oliver: Nos reuniremos en la nueva cafetería cerca del parque.

Mia: ¡Genial! Regresaré el domingo, así que quizá podamos vernos.

Oliver: ¡Perfecto! Tendremos una cena en mi casa esa noche.

Mia: ¡Maravilloso! Tengo muchas ganas.

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

## Main Uses

- Personal arrangements
- Fixed plans
- Organized events
- Travel arrangements

## Basic Structures

### Affirmative

Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing

### Negative

Subject + am/is/are + not + Verb-ing

### Question

Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing?

## Signal Words

- tonight
- tomorrow
- this weekend
- next week
- on Monday

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Use the Present Continuous for definite future arrangements.
> - The plan is usually already organized.
> - Use **am / is / are + verb-ing**.
> - Add a future time expression for clarity.
> - Do not confuse it with the Present Simple for schedules.
> - Use **will** for spontaneous decisions and **going to** for intentions.
