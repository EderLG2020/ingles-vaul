# 🧠 What Are Stative Verbs? / ¿Qué Son los Verbos Estativos?

Stative verbs describe a condition or state rather than an action.

Los verbos estativos describen una condición o estado en lugar de una acción.

They usually express:

- Feelings
- Thoughts
- Possession
- Senses
- Opinions
- Relationships

# 🚫 Stative Verbs and Present Continuous

Stative verbs are generally not used in the Present Continuous.

Los verbos estativos generalmente no se usan en Presente Continuo.

| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
|-------------|------------|
| I am knowing the answer. | I know the answer. |
| She is liking pizza. | She likes pizza. |
| They are owning a house. | They own a house. |
| We are believing you. | We believe you. |

---

# 🔄 Verbs That Can Be Both Stative and Dynamic

Some verbs change meaning depending on the context.

Algunos verbos cambian de significado según el contexto.

## Think

| Stative | Dynamic |
|----------|----------|
| I think you are right. (opinion) | I am thinking about my future. (mental activity) |

---

## Have

| Stative | Dynamic |
|----------|----------|
| I have a new car. (possession) | I am having lunch. (activity) |

---

## See

| Stative | Dynamic |
|----------|----------|
| I see a mountain. (vision) | I am seeing the doctor tomorrow. (meeting) |

---

## Taste

| Stative | Dynamic |
|----------|----------|
| The cake tastes delicious. | The chef is tasting the soup. |

---

## Smell

| Stative | Dynamic |
|----------|----------|
| The flowers smell nice. | She is smelling the perfume. |

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I am liking this movie. | I like this movie. |
>| She is knowing the answer. | She knows the answer. |
>| We are needing help. | We need help. |
>| They are owning a business. | They own a business. |
>| He is believing the story. | He believes the story. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - Stative verbs express a state, not an action.
> - Most stative verbs are not used with **-ing**.
> - Use the Simple Present for permanent states and feelings.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Lucas: Do you like classical music?

Sophia: Yes, I love it. I also like jazz.

Lucas: Do you know how to play an instrument?

Sophia: Yes, I know how to play the piano.

Lucas: I think music helps people relax.

Sophia: I agree. I believe it makes life better.

Lucas: Do you want to join the music club?

Sophia: Yes, I do. It sounds interesting.

Sophia: Sí. Suena interesante.

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Stative verbs usually do not use **-ing**.
> - Use the Simple Present with states and feelings.
> - Some verbs change meaning depending on the context.
> - Learn the most common stative verbs because they are used every day.
> - Do not say **"I am knowing"** or **"She is liking"**.
