
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** **Would** is a modal verb used to express **polite requests, hypothetical situations, preferences, repeated actions in the past, and conditional sentences**.
>
> **Español:** **Would** es un verbo modal que se usa para expresar **solicitudes corteses, situaciones hipotéticas, preferencias, acciones repetidas en el pasado y oraciones condicionales**.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - Would you help me?
> - I would like a coffee.
> - If I had money, I would travel the world.
> - When I was a child, I would play outside every day.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + would + Base Verb + Object**

### Contraction

**would = 'd**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I would help you. | Te ayudaría. |
| She would travel more. | Ella viajaría más. |
| They would understand. | Ellos entenderían. |
| I'd love to go. | Me encantaría ir. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + would not + Base Verb**

### Contraction

**would not = wouldn't**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I wouldn't do that. | Yo no haría eso. |
| She wouldn't lie to you. | Ella no te mentiría. |
| They wouldn't agree. | Ellos no estarían de acuerdo. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Would + Subject + Base Verb?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Would you help me? | ¿Me ayudarías? |
| Would she like some tea? | ¿Le gustaría un poco de té? |
| Would they come with us? | ¿Vendrían con nosotros? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, I would. | Sí. |
| No, I wouldn't. | No. |

# 📅 Uses of Would

## 1. Polite Requests

More polite than **can** or **will**.

Más cortés que **can** o **will**.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Would you open the window? | ¿Abrirías la ventana? |
| Would you help me, please? | ¿Me ayudarías, por favor? |

---

## 2. Preferences

Using **would like**.

Usando **would like**.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I would like some water. | Me gustaría un poco de agua. |
| She would like to travel. | A ella le gustaría viajar. |
| We'd like to order dinner. | Nos gustaría pedir la cena. |

---

## 3. Hypothetical Situations

Imaginary or unreal situations.

Situaciones imaginarias o irreales.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I would buy a house if I had money. | Compraría una casa si tuviera dinero. |
| She would be happier in another city. | Ella sería más feliz en otra ciudad. |

---

## 4. Conditional Sentences

Often used in the second and third conditional.

Se usa frecuentemente en el segundo y tercer condicional.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| If I won the lottery, I would travel the world. | Si ganara la lotería, viajaría por el mundo. |
| If they studied more, they would pass. | Si estudiaran más, aprobarían. |

---

## 5. Repeated Actions in the Past

Talking about habits in the past.

Hablar sobre hábitos en el pasado.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Every summer, we would visit our grandparents. | Cada verano visitábamos a nuestros abuelos. |
| My father would tell us stories. | Mi padre nos contaba historias. |

---

# 🔑 Common Expressions with Would

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| I would like... | Me gustaría... |
| Would you...? | ¿Podrías...? |
| I would love to... | Me encantaría... |
| I would rather... | Preferiría... |
| If I were you, I would... | Si fuera tú, yo... |

---

# ⚖️ Would vs Will

| Would | Will |
|---------|-------|
| Hypothetical | Real future |
| I would help you. | I will help you. |
| Polite request | Direct request |
| Would you open the door? | Will you open the door? |

---

# ⚖️ Would vs Used To

| Would | Used To |
|---------|----------|
| Past repeated actions | Past habits and states |
| We would play outside. | We used to live there. |
| Actions only | Actions and situations |

---

# 📍 Rules for Modal Verbs

After **would**, always use the base form of the verb.

Después de **would**, siempre se usa el verbo en su forma base.

### Correct

- I would help.
- She would study.
- They would wait.

### Incorrect

- I would to help.
- She would studies.
- They would waiting.

---

# 📍 Would Rather

**Would rather** expresses preference.

**Would rather** expresa preferencia.

### Examples

- I would rather stay home.
- She would rather walk.
- We'd rather wait.

---

# 📍 Would Have

**Would have + Past Participle** expresses unreal situations in the past.

**Would have + Participio Pasado** expresa situaciones irreales en el pasado.

### Examples

- I would have helped you.
- She would have come earlier.
- They would have won if they had practiced.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| I would to help you. | I would help you. |
>| She would likes coffee. | She would like coffee. |
>| Would you to come? | Would you come? |
>| They would going with us. | They would go with us. |
>| He woulds travel more. | He would travel more. |
>| I would like that you help me. | I would like you to help me. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - After **would**, always use the base form.
> - Never use **to** after **would**.
> - Modal verbs never take **-s**, **-ed**, or **-ing**.
> - **Would like** has its own structure: **would like + noun / to + verb**.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Emma: What would you do if you had a million dollars?

Ryan: I would travel around the world.

Emma: Really? I would buy a big house near the beach.

Ryan: That sounds great. Would you travel alone?

Emma: No, I would like to travel with my family.

Ryan: Me too. I would also help my parents.

Emma: That's nice. Would you stop working?

Ryan: Maybe not. I would rather work and enjoy life.

Emma: I agree. I would do the same.

Emma: ¿Qué harías si tuvieras un millón de dólares?

Emma: No, me gustaría viajar con mi familia.

Ryan: A mí también. También ayudaría a mis padres.

Emma: Qué bonito. ¿Dejarías de trabajar?

Ryan: Tal vez no. Preferiría trabajar y disfrutar la vida.

Emma: Estoy de acuerdo. Yo haría lo mismo.

- Polite requests
- Preferences
- Hypothetical situations
- Conditional sentences
- Repeated actions in the past

## Basic Structures

### Affirmative

Subject + would + Base Verb

### Negative

Subject + would not (wouldn't) + Base Verb

### Question

Would + Subject + Base Verb?

## Common Expressions

- I would like...
- Would you...?
- I would rather...
- I would love to...

> [!success] ⭐ Golden Rules
>
> - Use **would** for polite requests.
> - Use **would like** to express desires politely.
> - Use **would** in hypothetical and conditional situations.
> - Use **would** for repeated actions in the past.
> - After **would**, always use the base form of the verb.
> - Use **would have + past participle** for unreal past situations.
