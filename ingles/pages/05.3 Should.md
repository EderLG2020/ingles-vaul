
> [!tip] ⚡ Quick Rule / Regla Rápida
>
> **English:** **Should** is a modal verb used to give **advice, recommendations, opinions, and expectations**.
>
> **Español:** **Should** es un verbo modal que se usa para dar **consejos, recomendaciones, opiniones y expectativas**.
>
> **Examples:**
>
> - You should study more.
> - We should leave now.
> - She should see a doctor.
> - They should arrive soon.

# 🏗️ Basic Structure / Estructura Básica

## Affirmative / Afirmativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + should + Base Verb + Object**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| You should study every day. | Deberías estudiar todos los días. |
| She should exercise more. | Ella debería hacer más ejercicio. |
| We should help our parents. | Deberíamos ayudar a nuestros padres. |
| They should save money. | Ellos deberían ahorrar dinero. |

---

## Negative / Negativo

### Formula / Fórmula

**Subject + should not + Base Verb**

### Contraction

**should not = shouldn't**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| You shouldn't smoke. | No deberías fumar. |
| She shouldn't worry too much. | Ella no debería preocuparse demasiado. |
| They shouldn't be late. | Ellos no deberían llegar tarde. |

---

## Questions / Preguntas

### Formula / Fórmula

**Should + Subject + Base Verb?**

### Examples

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Should I call him? | ¿Debería llamarlo? |
| Should we wait? | ¿Deberíamos esperar? |
| Should they study more? | ¿Deberían estudiar más? |

### Short Answers

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| Yes, you should. | Sí. |
| No, you shouldn't. | No. |

# 📅 Uses of Should

## 1. Giving Advice

Giving personal recommendations.

Dar consejos personales.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| You should eat healthy food. | Deberías comer comida saludable. |
| She should sleep earlier. | Ella debería dormir más temprano. |

---

## 2. Making Recommendations

Suggesting the best option.

Sugerir la mejor opción.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| We should visit that museum. | Deberíamos visitar ese museo. |
| You should read this book. | Deberías leer este libro. |

---

## 3. Expressing Opinions

Saying what you think is right.

Decir lo que crees que es correcto.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| People should respect nature. | La gente debería respetar la naturaleza. |
| Everyone should learn English. | Todos deberían aprender inglés. |

---

## 4. Expressing Expectations

Talking about what is likely to happen.

Hablar sobre lo que probablemente sucederá.

| English | Español |
|----------|----------|
| The bus should arrive soon. | El autobús debería llegar pronto. |
| She should be home by now. | Ella ya debería estar en casa. |

# ⚖️ Should vs Must

| Should | Must |
|----------|-------|
| Advice | Obligation |
| You should study. | You must study. |
| Optional | Necessary |

---

# ⚖️ Should vs Ought To

| Should | Ought To |
|----------|------------|
| More common | More formal |
| You should rest. | You ought to rest. |
| Everyday English | Less common |

---

# 📍 Rules for Modal Verbs

After **should**, always use the base form of the verb.

Después de **should**, siempre se usa el verbo en su forma base.

### Correct

- You should study.
- She should go.
- They should wait.

### Incorrect

- You should to study.
- She should goes.
- They should waiting.

---

# 📍 Should Have

**Should have + Past Participle** is used to talk about past advice or regret.

**Should have + Participio Pasado** se usa para hablar de consejos pasados o arrepentimiento.

### Examples

- You should have called me.
- I should have studied more.
- They should have arrived earlier.

---

> [!warning] ⚠️ Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes
>
>| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
>|--------------|-------------|
>| You should to study. | You should study. |
>| She should goes home. | She should go home. |
>| Should I to call him? | Should I call him? |
>| They shouldn't smoking. | They shouldn't smoke. |
>| He shoulds work harder. | He should work harder. |
>
> ### Why? / ¿Por qué?
>
> - After **should**, always use the base form.
> - Never use **to** after **should**.
> - Modal verbs never take **-s**, **-ed**, or **-ing**.

# 🎭 Conversation Practice / Conversación

## English

Lucas: I have an English test tomorrow.

Sophia: You should study tonight.

Lucas: I know, but I'm very tired.

Sophia: You shouldn't stay up too late either.

Lucas: Should I review grammar or vocabulary first?

Sophia: I think you should review grammar and then practice vocabulary.

Lucas: That's a good idea. Should we study together?

Sophia: Sure! We should meet online at seven.

Lucas: Perfect. We should be ready for the test.

---

## Español

Lucas: Tengo un examen de inglés mañana.

Sophia: Deberías estudiar esta noche.

Lucas: Lo sé, pero estoy muy cansado.

Sophia: Tampoco deberías quedarte despierto hasta muy tarde.

Lucas: ¿Debería repasar gramática o vocabulario primero?

Sophia: Creo que deberías repasar gramática y luego practicar vocabulario.

Lucas: Es una buena idea. ¿Deberíamos estudiar juntos?

Sophia: Claro. Deberíamos reunirnos en línea a las siete.

Lucas: Perfecto. Deberíamos estar listos para el examen.
