# 🎭 Classroom Dialogue / Diálogo de Clase

## English

Teacher: Good morning, everyone.

Students: Good morning, teacher.

Teacher: Please open your books on page twenty.

Student: Excuse me, what page are we on?

Teacher: Page twenty.

Student: Thank you.

Teacher: Read the first paragraph, please.

Student: Excuse me, I don't understand this word.

Teacher: Which word?

Student: "Journey".

Teacher: It means "trip" or "travel".

Student: Thank you. How do you pronounce it?

Teacher: /ˈdʒɜːrni/

Student: Could you repeat that, please?

Teacher: Of course. Journey.

Student: Journey.

Teacher: Excellent!

---

## Español

Profesor: Buenos días a todos.

Estudiantes: Buenos días, profesor.

Profesor: Por favor, abran sus libros en la página veinte.

Estudiante: Disculpe, ¿en qué página estamos?

Profesor: Página veinte.

Estudiante: Gracias.

Profesor: Lean el primer párrafo, por favor.

Estudiante: Disculpe, no entiendo esta palabra.

Profesor: ¿Cuál palabra?

Estudiante: "Journey".

Profesor: Significa "viaje".

Estudiante: Gracias. ¿Cómo se pronuncia?

Profesor: /ˈdʒɜːrni/

Estudiante: ¿Podría repetirlo, por favor?

Profesor: Claro. Journey.

Estudiante: Journey.

Profesor: ¡Excelente!

# 📌 Summary / Resumen

### Essential Student Phrases

- I don't understand.
- Can you repeat that?
- How do you say ___ in English?
- What does this mean?
- How do you pronounce it?

### Essential Teacher Instructions

- Open your books.
- Listen carefully.
- Repeat after me.
- Read the text.
- Write the answer.

### Golden Rules ⭐

1. Don't be afraid to ask questions.
2. Learn classroom expressions early.
3. Use polite language (please, excuse me, thank you).
4. Practice survival phrases until they become automatic.
5. These expressions are useful in every English class.
